The What and How of my Web site

What will your Web site include (graphics, text, links, audio, video, etc.)?

It will definitely need pictures of the animals on the subpage of adoptable pets. Perhaps making some kind of logo graphic will be a good idea. Homepage will need a big eye catching image banner similar to that of Main Line Animal Rescue.

I suppose also some text is needed but it will probably be more image focused. The text will mainly be to describe the shelter, the history, and the pets.

The links will be to other information, other shelters, and news in general.
